MARWADI UNIVERSITY

Marwadi University is a private university located in Rajkot, Gujarat, India.
It was established on 9 May 2016 by the Marwadi Education Foundation through
The Gujarat Private Universities Act, 2016. As of 2017, it offers 54 different courses.
It is graded A+ by NAAC
